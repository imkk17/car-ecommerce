High-definition touchscreens, uninterrupted satellite connectivity, and an advanced navigation system ensure an optimal user experience. Ergonomic leather seats and premium materials offer unparalleled comfort.

    Thanks to Eutelsat's cutting-edge satellite technology, this car provides global connectivity, enabling seamless communication and entertainment wherever you are. Under the hood, it houses a powerful engine that combines performance and efficiency for a dynamic and responsive drive.

    This collaboration between Eutelsat and the automaker marks a new era for luxury cars, merging advanced connectivity with exceptional design. It promises a driving experience that combines the best of satellite technology and automotive luxury, offering users an unprecedented journey around the world.

    With this car, the future of connected mobility comes to life, opening up new possibilities and horizons for technology and automotive enthusiasts.
